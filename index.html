🎨 Text-to-Image Tips

  • Be concise but descriptive — specify main subject, style, lighting, and color mood
  • Put text you want rendered in "quotes" and describe typography style
  • Specify visual format: infographic, greeting card, product photo
  • Request three variations or four color palettes for side-by-side comparison
  • For character consistency: assign distinct names and upload clear reference images

✏️ Image-to-Image Editing

  • Upload 1–2 reference images and describe the edit: change background to sunset
  • Style transfer: upload an image + describe target style: make it look like a watercolor painting
  • Inpainting: describe what to add/remove: remove the person on the left
  • Combine 2 images: upload both and describe how to merge them
  • Localization: translate the text in the image to Japanese

🧠 Thinking Levels

  • Minimal (default) — fast generation, good for simple prompts
  • High — model reasons through complex prompts before rendering, better quality & prompt adherence

📐 Resolution Guide

  • Standard (1K) — balanced quality and speed
  • Medium (2K) — higher detail for print and web
  • HD (4K) — maximum quality, production-ready
